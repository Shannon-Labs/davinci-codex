### Operating Procedures

#### Normal Startup Procedure
```
1. Complete pre-operation safety checklist
2. Ensure all personnel are clear of rotating machinery
3. Verify emergency stop devices are accessible
4. Start water flow gradually (increase over 30 seconds)
5. Monitor initial operation for unusual conditions
6. Check for proper rotation and smooth operation
7. Verify power output is within specification
8. Document all operating parameters

Critical Parameters to Monitor:
- Rotational speed: 24 ¬± 1 RPM
- Power output: 358 ¬± 20 Watts
- Bearing temperature: <70¬∞C
- Vibration levels: <4.5mm/s RMS
- Hydraulic pressure: <200 kPa
- Noise levels: <85 dB(A)
```

#### Normal Shutdown Procedure
```
1. Reduce water flow gradually (over 30 seconds)
2. Allow wheel to coast to complete stop
3. Engage emergency stop if unusual conditions observed
4. Close all water supply valves
5. Allow system to drain completely
6. Perform post-operation inspection
7. Document any abnormalities or maintenance needs
8. Secure the system against unauthorized use
```

#### Emergency Shutdown Procedure
```
1. IMMEDIATELY engage emergency stop device
2. Close main water supply valve
3. Clear all personnel from immediate area
4. Assess situation for safety hazards
5. Contact emergency services if injuries occur
6. Document emergency circumstances
7. Do not restart until safety inspection completed
8. Report incident to safety authority
```
