### Pillar 1: **The Living Codex**
Transform static documentation into an **interactive manuscript**:

- **Folio-by-Folio Navigation**: Browse inventions as Leonardo would have organized them
- **Provenance Trails**: Click any component to see its source folio, transcription, and historical context
- **Evolution Timeline**: Watch how Leonardo's ideas evolved across different codices

- **3D Interactive Models**: Rotate, explode, animate every mechanism in real-time

- **Failure Analysis**: Learn from what didn't work‚ÄîLeonardo's "failures" were often his greatest teachers

- **Acoustic Visualization**: See sound waves, harmonics, and resonance patterns
- **Historical Repertoire**: Recreate music from Leonardo's era
- **Modern Adaptations**: Bridge Renaissance music to contemporary compositions

### Pillar 4: **The Flight Laboratory**
Make aviation inventions feel like a **working flight test facility**:

- **Real-Time Flight Simulator**: Fly the ornithopter, control the aerial screw
- **Wind Tunnel Visualization**: See airflow, vortices, and pressure distributions
- **Biomimetic Analysis**: Compare Leonardo's designs to actual bird flight
- **Safety Scenarios**: Interactive parachute deployment, emergency procedures
- **Performance Dashboards**: Live telemetry during simulations

### Pillar 5: **The Innovation Engine**
Turn the project into a **discovery platform**:

- **Pattern Recognition**: AI-assisted discovery of overlooked inventions in the codices
- **Modern Applications**: Show how Leonardo's principles apply to contemporary problems
- **Collaborative Research**: Connect historians, engineers, and makers
- **Educational Pathways**: Structured learning journeys from beginner to expert
- **Impact Stories**: Document how the project inspires real-world innovation
