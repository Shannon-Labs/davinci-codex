Engineering Innovation

  • Central Pivot System: Lightweight wooden structure rotates on central pier
  • Counterweight Balance: Enables single-person operation of 15-meter span

Historical Context

Designed during Leonardo's service to Cesare Borgia as chief military engineer (1502-1503), the revolving bridge solved a critical tactical problem: rapid river crossing without permanent structures. The design appeared in Codex Atlanticus and represents Leonardo's genius for practical military innovation.
